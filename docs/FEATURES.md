# EverythingBeer Features

## Core Features

### 1. Beer Database
Browse our extensive collection of 65+ beers from craft breweries and commercial brands.

**Beer Information Includes:**
- Name and brewery
- Style classification
- ABV (Alcohol By Volume)
- IBU (International Bitterness Units)
- Flavor profile descriptions
- Tasting notes
- Food pairing suggestions
- Availability information

**Filtering Options:**
- **All Beers** - Complete database
- **Craft Beers** - Independent craft brewery selections
- **Commercial Beers** - Major brand offerings
- **Trending Beers** - Popular and highly-rated selections
- **By Style** - Filter by IPA, Stout, Lager, etc.
- **By Origin** - American, Belgian, German, etc.

### 2. Brewery Directory
Explore breweries from around the world with detailed profiles.

**Brewery Information:**
- Name and location
- Founding year
- Brewery type (micro, regional, large)
- Featured beers
- Contact information
- Website links

### 3. Beer Styles Guide
Comprehensive guide to 40+ beer styles with detailed characteristics.

**Style Categories:**
- Ales (IPA, Pale Ale, Stout, Porter, etc.)
- Lagers (Pilsner, Bock, MÃ¤rzen, etc.)
- Wheat Beers (Hefeweizen, Witbier, etc.)
- Belgian Styles (Dubbel, Tripel, Saison, etc.)
- Sour Beers (Gose, Berliner Weisse, etc.)
- Specialty Beers (Fruit, Smoked, etc.)

**Style Information:**
- Origin and history
- Flavor characteristics
- ABV and IBU ranges
- Serving temperature
- Glassware recommendations
- Example beers

### 4. Beer Comparison Tool
Compare up to 4 beers side-by-side to make informed decisions.

**Comparison Metrics:**
- ABV comparison
- IBU comparison
- Flavor profiles
- Price points
- Availability
- User ratings

### 5. Beer Finder Quiz
Interactive quiz to discover new beers based on your preferences.

**Quiz Factors:**
- Flavor preferences (hoppy, malty, fruity, etc.)
- Bitterness tolerance
- Alcohol preference
- Previous favorites
- Occasion (casual, special, food pairing)

### 6. Educational Guides
Learn everything about beer with our comprehensive guides.

**Available Guides:**
- Beer Tasting 101 - How to properly taste and evaluate beer
- Food Pairing Guide - Best beer and food combinations
- Brewing Basics - Understanding the brewing process
- Glassware Guide - Choosing the right glass
- Beer Storage - Proper storage and aging
- Craft Beer Movement - History and culture

### 7. Mega Menu Navigation
Quick access to all sections with smart categorization.

**Menu Sections:**
- Beers (All, Craft, Commercial, Trending)
- Breweries (By region and type)
- Styles (By category)
- Learn (Guides and resources)
